FFT & Speech Recognition

  • Date: 2020 - 2021

  • Name: Investigation in the derivation of Fast Fourier Transform and its application in speech recognition

  • Description: In daily conversation, human ears convert sound vibrations into electric signals that are recognisable by the brain. However, computers lack this method of interpretation; thus a computational algorithm Fast Fourier Transform (FFT) is applied. This makes me ponder: how is FFT used in speech recognition? To answer my research question, this essay will examine the application of FFT on the two fundamental features of speech recognition: noise reduction and vowel identification.
